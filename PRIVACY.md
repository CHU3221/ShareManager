# Privacy Policy for ShareManager

**Effective Date: July 4, 2026**

## 1. Information Collection and Use
ShareManager is a local-first, open-source file sharing utility. **We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information, telemetry data, or file contents to any external servers or third parties.** All network operations occur exclusively between your configured local Windows client and your designated personal server.

## 2. Local Data Storage
Any configurations, such as server IP addresses or API keys, are stored locally on your device. The application may generate local log files for debugging purposes. These files remain entirely on your personal computer and are never automatically uploaded or shared.

## 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this repository.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us by creating an issue on our GitHub repository.
